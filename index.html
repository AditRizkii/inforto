05

How the Detection System Works

This smart urinal combines two sensors to automatically detect health conditions from urine samples — no blood tests or clinic visits required.

🚶
User Detection
An infrared sensor detects the user. The control valve closes to retain urine for analysis (100ml capacity).
1
⚗️
pH H-101 Sensor
Accurately measures urine acidity. A very low (acidic) or high (alkaline) pH can indicate metabolic disorders, kidney disease, or infection.
2
🎨
TCS3200 Color Sensor
Reads the RGB color values of urine. Dark brown, orange, cloudy, or red hues each point to different conditions in specific organs.
3
🧠
Arduino Processing
The Arduino Nano processes pH and RGB data simultaneously. pH corrects any color reading errors caused by food dyes or medications.
4
🖥️
Result Display
Status is shown on an LCD screen in under 30 seconds. Results can be used as the basis for a conversation with a healthcare provider.
5
💧
Auto-Flush
After the user leaves, the valve opens and the pump flushes the urinal for 8 seconds, keeping it clean for the next user.
